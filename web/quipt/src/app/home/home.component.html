What is it?

A video board app. You can upload short videos and make collections (boards) out of them.

What can you do with it?

  • Create boards
  • Upload videos to the boards
  • Caption the videos
  • Delete the videos and boards
  • Favorite boards

What are the limitations?

  • Videos must be in a playable format e.g. mov, mp4, etc
  • Must be less than 120 seconds in length
  • Must be 100 MB and under
